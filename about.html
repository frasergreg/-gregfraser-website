I've Been in Your Shoes

I'm Greg Fraser, a certified executive coach with 20+ years of tech leadership experience. I know what it's like to navigate the pressure, complexity, and loneliness of executive leadership because I've lived it.

What Makes Me Different

I bring the rare combination of deep operational experience as a 3x Chief Product Officer, Wharton MBA, and certified coach. I understand strategy, cross-functional collaboration, team building, and what it takes to scale. Not just in theory, but from doing it myself.

I'm a change catalyst who helps leaders identify their true goals, root challenges, and limiting assumptions, and respond to feedback.

My Coaching Philosophy

I help ambitious leaders grow their authentic leadership and become more resourceful. I believe every leader has the wisdom and capacity inside them to solve their biggest challenges. My role is to help you access that wisdom, see your blind spots, evolve your mindset, and take bold action.

I'm holistic in my approach: mind and body, professional and personal. I'm warm and supportive, yet playfully provocative—I'll challenge you with humor and honesty. Because real growth happens when you feel both challenged and supported.
